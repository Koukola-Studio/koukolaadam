Pecha Kucha Night Šumperk

— Visual Identity

— Pecha Kucha is a community event originally from Japan that has grown worldwide. It is currently hosted in more than 1,000 cities around the world. Šumperk is one of the cities that have the licence and privilege to host this event. Currently, Šumperk is hosting the 19th annual Pecha Kucha Night and I had the pleasure of creating a new visual identity for this year's event.

Preparation

— 01

— The visual identity is made of graphic elements that complement each other and can be combined into multiple variations.

— Each element has a minimalist person who has a specific background behind it. Each background suggests the story and experience of each of the speakers and visitors, who want to share their knowledge and inspire others through this.

Final Look

— 02

Conclusion

— 03

— In the visual identity, the combination of all elements into one grid should target the main point of the whole event - visitors will receive new experiences and inspirations from the speakers who will be speaking at this event
